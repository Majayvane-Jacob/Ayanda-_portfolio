About-Me



I am Ayanda Jacob and my name means growth. I am 20 years of age and I was born in Cape Town on July 12th 1998. I am the last born out of 4 kidds(2 brother and a sister) from my mother but from dad its a different story. I would say I come from a hard working, previldged and business minded family but most people say and think im rich beacause my brothers own all 3 of my parents businesses now that they retired. But as I would say to people im not the rich one my parents and brothers are. Poeple have a tendency to think money can buy everything in the world forgetting that happiness and trust can never be bought. As im the last born people love saying im a spoilt brat but really no one knows what happens in the 4 corners of No.1 Bhunga Ave, only the people living there do. Growing up I never had anything easy, everything I have now I worked extremely hard for and i thank my mother and father for that mainly because whenever i asked for something i had to work for it. Example (wash all the cars, mats , windows or offer to wash my brothers shoes in exchange for money). Only 1 person gave me money from time to time for no reason (he still does till this day) and he is my role model, my brother Phumzile Nelson Jacob. Ok ok enough about my past. Im currently a student at Quirky 30 and Im studying Web Development, Graphic Design and Digital Marketing. Im a Event Organiser/Dancer/Photographer, i own an event company with a group of friends call ourselves AB Entertainment. We host the Littest and craziest parties every weekend at my brother's shop Malusi's Meat to give his business recognition. So all in all i would say Ayanda is very passionate, humble, strong person and loves seing others smile. My perpose in life is to make others happy and change lives.
